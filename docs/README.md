# 🐄 Sakeng Livestock

> **Lesotho's Trusted Livestock Marketplace**

🌐 Production: https://sakenglivestock.com

Sakeng Livestock is a mobile-first digital marketplace that connects livestock buyers and sellers across Lesotho. The platform exists to make livestock trading more transparent, accessible and trustworthy while reducing reliance on informal channels and cross-border sourcing.

This repository contains both the **production MVP** and the **documentation that guides its continued evolution**.

# 🎯 Mission

To build the trusted digital infrastructure for livestock trading in Lesotho by making it easier for farmers, buyers and businesses to discover, buy and sell quality livestock.

---

# 🌍 The Problem

Livestock is the lifeblood of many Basotho families, yet the industry faces several challenges:

- No central livestock marketplace.
- Heavy dependence on South African suppliers.
- Limited access to quality livestock.
- Informal trading through Facebook and word of mouth.
- Lack of reliable livestock data and market insights.

Sakeng exists to address these challenges by creating a secure and structured marketplace built specifically for Lesotho.
